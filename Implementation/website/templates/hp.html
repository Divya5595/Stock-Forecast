Stock Market Prediction using Support Vector Machine

Problem Statement

Stock Prediction has been considered as both, an interesting and a difficult research topic in the field of finance and engineering due to its potential financial gain.

Many researchers have strived for proving the predictability of the financial market by doing fundamental analysis or technical analysis, but to an accuracy of 85-89%.

Our aim is to predict the future stock prices accurately using supervised learning algorithms. This will be achieved by building a software that analyses historical stock data.
